Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Nicole Huynh

Drama version

Interior: School hallway, early morning. Marcia (15) walks through the hallways.

(Sound: Distant sounds of chatting and locker slams.)

Marcia: (to self) Why is it so quiet today? Oh, there is a group over there. I should ask them.

(Marcia walks up to a group of students talking)

Marcia: Why is it so quiet? Is anything going on today?

(George (14) stops chatting and looks over to Marcia)

George: (looks at their wrist) Gotta go, guys come on!

Marcia: Wait! Wait, can you answer my question? Please.

George: (looks over at Marcia) Sorry, I have to go.

(The rest of the group follows George)

Marcia: (to self) That was weird. Oh There’s Mrs. Smith. Hi Mrs. Smith.

(Mrs. Smith moves her head in an inhuman manner)

Mrs. Smith: (cheerly) Oh hi, Marcia.

(Mrs. Smith acts strange. She acts very happy to the point, it's inhuman)

Marcia: (suspicious) I’ll… be going now. Hope you have a good day!

Mrs. Smith: See you later.

(Marcia speedwalks away.)

Marcia: (looks around – to self) Something is wrong. Something is definitely wrong. Mrs. Smith would definitely talk more. It’ll be fine. It’ll be fine.

(Marcia sees someone that looks exactly like Mrs. Smith in the distance)

(Sound: Chatting starts to quiet down as a teacher’s yelling is heard.)

Marcia: (runs up to her) Wait, you look exactly like Mrs. Smith?

Mrs. Smith: Oh hi, Marcia.

Marcia: Mrs. Smith, weren't you over there-

School Announcement: Watch out for skinwalkers! Don’t trust anyone nearby. Be safe, students and teachers.

(Sound: The sound of students chatting and locker slamming disappears instantly.)

Marcia looks around, and there were no one but her and Mrs. Smith.

Marcia: (shocked) Y-you aren’t Mrs. Smith.

Not Mrs. Smith: (smiles) Oh hi, Marcia.

(Not Mrs. Smith changes and Marcia shrieks)

THE END
