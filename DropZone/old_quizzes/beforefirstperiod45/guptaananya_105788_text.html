Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Ananya Gupta

Drama Version: “Before First Period”

Interior: School hallway, early morning. Millie (15) walks along a back hallway with her books clutched in her arms protectively.

(Sound: Lockers clanging in the distance. Footsteps echo.)

Millie: (quietly to self) Why does it feel so empty?

(Millie goes to a group of girls chattering in the hallway)

Millie: Hi, what’s going on?

Samantha: What do you mean? Nothing’s going on.

Millie: Why is it so empty?

Carrie: Um, it’s not.

Gina: We have to go now.

Millie: Wait! Where are you going?

Samantha: The cafeteria!

Carrie: (whispering) That girl is so weird.

Gina: Yeah, it’s not empty, everyone hasn’t come yet.

(The group of girls go to the cafeteria, Millie is confused)

(Sound: Footsteps increase, the buses start coming)

(Millie walks up to her teacher that she greets every morning, Mr. Williams)

Millie: Hello Mr. Williams, how are you doing?

Mr. Williams: (In a distant tone) I’m doing fine.

Millie: Okay.

(Millie walks in the hall to see another teacher about an assignment)

(Sound: Footsteps fading)

(Millie spots her class and runs there)

(Sound: Footsteps increasing)

Mr. Thompson: Millie!

(Millie immediately stops and looks back)

Millie: Oh, hello Mr. Thompson, how are you doing?

Mr. Thompson: (In the same distant tone) I’m doing fine. Now, why were you running in the hallway?

Millie: I was trying to get to Mrs. Rose’s class; I have to ask her about an assignment.

(Millie notices the same outfit is on Mr. Thompson)

Millie: You and Mr. Williams are wearing the same outfit.

Mr. Thompson: (In the same distant tone as before) Today is uniform day for all teachers and staff members.

(Principal on the intercom)

(Sound: Intercom)

Principal Sherman: (In a calm voice) Hello students, they are back, stay inside the building and you will all be safe.

(The announcement ends; Millie is confused)

(Sound: Intercom stops, everything is silent)

Millie: (In a scared voice) Mr. Thompson, what is happening?

(Footsteps approaching, then stop at Mr. Thompson)

Mr. Thompson: (In normal voice) Mille, hide.

(Millie runs into the janitor’s closet and almost cries.)

Mysterios Voice: Mr. Thompson, where is Millie?

Mr. Thompson: (In a distant voice) In the janitor’s closet.

(Footsteps approaching, door opening)

Millie: (In a scared voice) What do you want from me?

Mysterious Voice: You’re next.

(Millie screams as they take her.)

Millie: AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAH!
