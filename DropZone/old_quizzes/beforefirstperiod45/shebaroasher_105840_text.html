Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Asher Shebaro

(Marcia, a Ukrainian girl at the age of 14, walks into the hall, she is wearing a backpack and holding books. She looks nervous and confused) 

(before class) 

(Marcia walks up to a group of students) 

Marcia: (anxious) Hiiiii. I’m Marcia, I’m from Ukraine. 

Student 1: (in a bored tone) Cool. 

Student 2:(in a bored tone) Nice. 

Student 3: We have to go. 

(Marcia tries to speak but the students move off stage) 

Marcia: That was weird. 

(A teacher and a student walk on stage talking in a heavy tone) 

Student 4: Hi 

Teacher 1: Hi 

Student 4: Is it almos-. 

(The student and the teacher stop talking immediately when Marcia walks up) 

(They quickly walk away) 

Marcia: (confused) What... 

(Marcia continues to walk when she encounters another adult that looks like the first teacher) 

Marcia: Hi teacher, this is my first day, what’s-? 

(Marcia gets cut off by the principal on the loudspeaker) 

Mr. Barcelona: Good morning everybody, today we just have a minor safety announcement. When you are using the thing, make sure you wear proper safety equipment. 

(The teachers walk the students off until it is just Marcia and the teacher next to her) 

Marcia: (confused) What is going on in this crazy school?! 

Teacher 2: (In a menacing tone) Come with me. 

(BLACKOUT)
