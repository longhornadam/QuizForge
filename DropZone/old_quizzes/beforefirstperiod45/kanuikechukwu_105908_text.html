Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Ikechukwu Kanu

DRAMA VERSION: “Before First Period”

Interior: School hallway, Early morning. Michael (13) Arrives with a backpack

(Sound: Footsteps)

MICHAEL: (to self) That’s weird, it’s quieter than usual.

(MICHAEL shrugs it off and starts heading to his first period.)

(MICHAEL passes by a small group of students.)

MICHAEL: (to the group of students) Hello

STUDENTS: (In a calm voice) Hello

(The group of students turned away from MICHAEL and started walking)

MICHAEL: Wait!

(The students didn’t seem to hear him. They kept walking without a flinch.)

MICHAEL: (to self) What is happening today??

(MICHAEL walks off, heading for his first period, then encounters a familiar teacher)

(MICHEAL spoke first)

MICHAEL: Hi, Mr. Johnson!

  1. JOHNSON (40s): (In a calm voice with a straight face) Hello, Michael. Have a good day today.

MICHAEL: Uhm…, ok!

(MICHAEL keeps walking, trying to calm himself by focusing on a familiar routine.)

(The other students around him look unconcerned)

(MICHAEL finds a teacher he had never met before. He looked almost exactly like MR. JOHNSON)

(MICHAEL turned around to confirm if MR. JOHNSON was still there)

(MICHAEL turns back around.)
MICHAEL: (to the teacher, points out) Hello! You look almost exactly like MR. JOHNSON. I’ve never met you before.

Teacher (???): (In the same way as MR. JOHNSON) Hello, Michael. Have a good day today.

MICHAEL: (confused) Huh? Why did you say exactly what Mr. Johnson said, and how do you know my-

(A school announcement interrupts.)

VOICE OVER LOUDSPEAKER: (calm but serious) Students, there is a bomb detected on the campus. Please exit safely and calmly.

MICHAEL: (Worried) What?!

(MICHAEL turns and starts looking around. He sees the students being guided by the teachers, but they all had a calm expression.)

(MICHAEL starts hearing a beeping sound seemingly from his backpack.)

MICHAEL (coming to a realization): Oh no..
