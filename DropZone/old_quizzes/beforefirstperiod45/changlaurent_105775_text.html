Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Laurent Chang

Interior: School Hallway

Charlie(student)

Mrs. Crawn (teacher)

Jack : Charlie’s friend

(Charlie walks down the school hallway earlier than usual)

(Sound: Sharp noises more amplified than usual)

Charlie: (stops) How are you guys doing?

Students: Great!

(Charlie takes a few steps before he stops to a halt seeing Mrs. Crawn)

Charlie: Hi Mrs. Crawn! How’s your day?

Mrs. Crawn: Good.

No expression just pure words

(Charlie walks more before he sees… Mrs. Crawn again????)

Charlie: H- Hi? I thought you were just down there?

Mrs. Crawn: Great.

No expression just pure words

Charlie: I am terrified of what’s happening. For whatever reason I can’t-

Mrs. Crawn: Charlie come here, Charlie come here.

(Charlie walks to Mrs. Crawn looking at her)

Charlie: Yes?

(Mrs. Crawn was never there)

(Now, with terror in mind about his reality, does she know it’s really his world?)

Charlie: (stops walking) Why are they acting so…, odd?

(Charlie heads to class feeling uneasy)

(As Charlie gets the courage to walk into the classroom, she sees…)

Mrs. Crawn: Hello there Charlie. (Her face filled with joy)

Charlie: H- Hi Mrs. Crawn.

Mrs. Crawn: (points to a desk in the middle of the class) That’ll be your seat next to Jack.

Charlie heads to his seat putting his backpack behind his chair.

Charlie: Let's do this.

 

 

(END)
