Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Aubrey Cunningham

Drama Version: "Before First Period"

Interior: School hallway, early morning. Harry Rington (13) walks along a back hallway.

(Sound: Lockers clanging in the distance. Footsteps echo.)

Harry: (quietly - to self) Why does it feel so empty?

(On the way to his locker, he passes a group of friends.)

Harry: Hey, Adam, are we still meeting up at 5:00 today?

(Sound: snickering and giggling)

Adam: (snickering) No, I'm busy with my science project.

Harry: (dejected) Oh...John, you still wanted to play basketball on Saturday, right?

John: (disgusted) No thanks. Let's go guys!

Harry: (desperate) Wait-

(The group struts away, leaving a trail of disgust and rudeness behind them.)

(Harry continues to walk towards his locker)

Harry: (to self) Why won't anyone talk to me?

Student 1: (laughing) Talking to yourself Rington? That's a new low, even for you!

(Before Harry can react, the student had already walked away.)

Harry: (thinking) Something feels wrong.

(Harry finally made it to his first class, after putting his bag in his locker and looting the vending machine for snacks.)

(Sound: quiet chatter of students, echo of footsteps, and the ringing bell.)

Harry: (smiling) Hi Coach Rassberri!

Coach Rassberri: (blank face) Hello Harry. Please go take your seat.

Harry: (thinking) That's a bit...harsh for Coach Rassberri...is she alright?

Harry: (normal expression) Are you alright Coach Rassberri?

Coach Rassberri: (sternly) Please. Take. Your. Seat.

(Harry finally takes his seat, although he can't stop thinking about how Coach's movements, tone, and face seemed...wrong)

Coach Rassberri: (smiling) Class is now dismissed. Please exit the room.

(Harry stumbles out of his seat, eager to leave)

(Sound: Steps echo throughout the hallway, kids chatter, and lockers slam shut)

Harry: (thoughts) Everyone seems unbothered, so I should do what I normally do.

(Harry started walking towards the reading hallway for his next class. He even forgot about the incident for a minute.)

Coach Rassberri: (smiling) Hello, Harry. Having fun?

Harry: (frowning) Didn't I just see you outside of your classroom?

Coach Rassberri: (no expression) Please go take your seat.

(Just then an announcement played over the loudspeaker.)

Loudspeaker: (yelling) CODE RED, CODE RED!

Harry: (thoughts) Code Red means we need to evacuate!

Loudspeaker: (yelling) THEY GOT IN! EVERYBODY HIDE!

(For a moment, everybody was silent. Then it happened.)

(Sound: Kids screaming and loud footsteps)

Harry: (confused) What's going on?

Coach Rassberri: (smiling) Please, come in and take your seat.

Harry: (scared) We're supposed to evacuate though!

Coach Rassberri: (angry) *grabs his arm* You must take your seat.

Harry: *pulls his hand away* What's wrong with you?

(Sound: Teachers leading scared students to places to hide)

Harry: (thoughts) She's...one of them. 

(Darkness begins to close in on them)

Harry: (realization) Something's very wrong here.

(Harry sprints down the seemingly endless hall, and tries to open every door he sees.)

(Sound: Door knobs rattling and one pair of footsteps echoing down the long hall)

Coach Rassberri: (smiling) You can't run from us anymore.

(Harry didn't try to run; there was nothing he could do since he'd reached the end of the hallway.)

Coach Rassberri: I've finally caught up.

(CUT TO BLACK)

THE END
