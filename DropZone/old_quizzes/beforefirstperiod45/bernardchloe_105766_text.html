Before First Period

Interior: A Middle School, 7:45 A.M. 

Sounds of footsteps, chattering and laughing echoed sharply into MARIA 's (13) ears although it was quiet. A heavy quiet, like if someone died. She walks towards the gym, encountering a teacher in a beige suit and black hair tied in a ponytail.

MARIA: (Smiling) Good Morning.

TEACHER 1: (Relays the smile a bit too wide with a robotic tone) Good morning.

MARIA's smile faded for a moment and her brow twitched but she shook her head and continued on, encountering a group of students.

MARIA: Hi! 

STUDENT 1: (Similar robotic tone) Hi.

STUDENT 2: (Waves)

MARIA: Hmm... Do you guys like... Pokemon?

STUDENT 1: (Unblinking) Maybe.

The group of students shuffle away without a second glance.

Maria, confused and butthurt, continued walking to gym class.

MARIA: (Glancing down at a squished bug) Oh! Poor spider... Looks kinda weird. (Takes out phone and takes a picture) Cool. 

MARIA encounters another teacher along the way. The spanish teacher. They look the same as the first teacher... Shrugging away the similarities, MARIA greets the spanish teacher.

MARIA: Hola senor!

TEACHER 2: (Staring at the spot she standing in a few seconds ago) Hello Maria.

MARIA: (Awkwardly backs up to where he was staring) I should go... I don't want to be late!... Heh...

Maria wonders what the hell was happening as she speedily walked through the hallways with her head down.

MARIA: (Quietly to self) That can't be a coincidence...

Maria was cut off by an announcement.

SCHOOL INTERCOM: LOCKDOWN, LOCKDOWN. SHUT OFF ALL LIGHTS AND LOCK THE DOORS.

Silence. Stillness. Suddenly all together kids all around Maria robotically moved into classrooms, herded by their even more robotic teachers and doors were shutting all around her.

MARIA: (Urgent and panicked) No! No! Wait for me! 

MARIA: Uh oh-...

Fades to black.
