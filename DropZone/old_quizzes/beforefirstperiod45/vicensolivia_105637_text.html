Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Olivia Vicens

School cafeteria, clock reads 7:30, Izra(13) walks around the tables, heaving her backpack behind her, 

Regular school sounds, chattering, and few students scattered throughout the cafeteria. Loud for the amount of students present. 

IZRA: (passing by a group of 3 girls) Hi Emily! (waves)  

Emily waves back. Hesitantly and awkwardly. 

IZRA: Ughh... Did you finish the homework for Mr. Harrison? 

EMILY: Yeah. 

IZRA: (Relieved) Can I copy? I didn’t have ANY time last night. 

EMILY: Uhhh. Yeah I guess. (hurriedly adds) But not right now, in third period. 

Group walks away 

IZRA: But Emily- (group is far away now) I have Mr. Harrison second period! 

Izra walks away as well. She runs in to her ELAR teacher, Mrs. Fletzel (FLEE-T-Z-UHL) 

IZRA: Mrs. Fletzel! How are you? 

MRS. FLETZEL: (stern and strict face, emotionless, stops and peers over her glasses to look down at Izra) Good. 

Mrs. Fletzel abruptly walks off. Izra keeps walking towards the library where she usually went to gather with her friends before school. 

Inside the library, Izra is confused. Mrs. Fletzel is near the fantasy bookshelf, labeling books, as if she’s been there for hours. 

IZRA: Mrs. Fletzel? How did you get here? I could’ve sworn I just saw you a few seconds ago, walking the opposite direction (slows down) from.. Here... 

MRS. FLETZEL: (same face, and same tone as before, stern and strict) I have no clue what your little mouth is blabbering on about. You must be out of your mind, thinking I can be in two places at once, goodness gracious child. (exasperated sigh and annoyance) 

IZRA: (opens her mouth to speak but is interrupted by the crackling of the intercom turning on.) 

The voice on the intercom is very fuzzy, and static, more than usual. 

INTERCOM: Good mor-rning stu-u-u-dents, i-i-f you-u-u cou-u-ul-d-d-d a-a-a-ll ple-e-e-ase re-e-e-eport to the cafeteria-a-a  at thi-i-is ti-i-i-ime, there have been some...(long pause)...complications, including today’s sche-e-edule 

(Unsettling silence.) 

IZRA: (confused, turns to look at Mrs. Fletzel, Mrs. Fletzel is gone. Izra is shocked and confused) Mrs. Fletzel?? (louder) Mrs. Fletzel?! 

Mrs. Fletzel is no where to be seen. 

IZRA walks out of the library and back to the cafeteria where she sees Mrs. Fletzel walking in a strict, flat, and controlled way, on the left side of the cafeteria. 

She also sees the same person on the right side of the cafeteria. 

Izra is very confused. 

IZRA: (to herself)  Am I seeing double?? Or is there actually two Mrs. Fletzels??? 

Three more Mrs. Fletzels walk into the cafeteria. They all walk at the same exact pace, with the same, identical, stern, strict, glare. 

IZRA: Mrs. Fletzel!! Mrs. Fletzel?! 

They all turn to look at Izra and peer down at her in unison. 

IN UNISON: Yes dear? 

IZRA: (terrified) W-W-What's going on? 

(silence) 

Izra quickly turns around to see four teachers (who are all Mrs. Fletzel) creeping towards her.  

Izra turns around to start running away but sees a group of seven children being taken away. 

She recognizes one of them as Emily. 

IZRA: EMILY!! EMILY??!! 

Izra runs of stage in fear, trembling, running away as the curtains close.
