Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Enyou Wu

Interior: School hallway, early morning, Crystalbel (16) just arrived to school. 

(Sound: lockers clanging loudly) 

Crystalbel: Why is it so silent today? 

(Everybody rushing to get to class) 

Crystalbel: Hi Maya, how are you? 

Maya:(sounding rushed) Fine. 

(Maya quickly runs off) 

(Crystalbel makes it to math class) 

Mr.Barsley:(emotionless) Today we are going to work on the worksheet that I’m about to give you. 

(Mr.Barsley gives out the worksheet) 

Crystalbel:(relieved) At least my class is acting normal. 

(Crystalbel accidently knocked over her water bottle) 

(The class had no reaction at all) 

Crystalbel:(talking to herself) Huh, that’s weird, usually people laugh at me. 

(Few minutes pass by) 

(Crystalbel tapping her classmate) 

Crystalbel:(whispering) Hey Mariya, do you know the answer to question five? 

(Mariya doesn’t respond) 

(20 minutes pass by) 

(Crystalbel raises her hand) 

Crystalbel: Mr.Barsley may I go to the restroom? 

Mr.Barsley:(emotionless) Yes, you may. 

(Crystalbel walks out of class and sees a teacher) 

Crystalbel: Hi, Mrs. Smith. 

(Mrs. Smith didn’t say anything back) 

(Crystalbel looking at her face closely to see if it is Mrs. Smith) 

Crystalbel:(talking to herself) Woah, that is not Mrs. Smith it kind of looks like Mr. Barsley’s face. 

(Crystalbel just made it back to class when the loudspeaker went off) 

The principle:(emotionless) Emergency, emergency please stay in your classrooms until I say so. 

Crystalbel:(confused) What the huh is happening right now? 

(Crystalbel started to shake Mariya, when she noticed she also had the same face as Mr. Barsley and Mrs. Smith.) 

(Crystalbel trying to get out of the classroom) 

(The students and Mr. Barsley started ganging up on Crystalbel) 

Crystalbel:(screaming) HELP!!!!!!! 

The End
