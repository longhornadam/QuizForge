Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Naisha Ashraf

 

Drama Version: “Before First Period” 

Interior: Jameson High, school hallway, early morning. Y/N (15) walks along the side of the bustling hallway close to the lockers, they are trying to get to class, books held protectively against their chest.  

{Please note that Y/N means Your Name and is in no way, shape, or form directed at anyone particular.} 

 

Scene 1 Act 1 

Y/N:(to self) Why are there so many people...it’s usually not this crowded... 

(Sound: Bustling hallway, loud chatter from students and teachers, sneakers scuffing the floors as they walk to their respective places) 

(Y/N bumps into Jackson and his friends and drops their books) 

(Sound: Conversation fading from Jackson’s friend group) 

Y/N: Sorry Jackson... (they mumble quietly, dropping to the floor to pick up the books) 

Jackson: (monotone) It is fine dude...don’t stress it. 

(Jackson bends over to help Y/N pick up their books, but his movements are oddly jerky...like he’s being controlled like a puppet) 

Y/N : (cheerfully) Thanks Jackson!  

Jackson: (monotone) You are welcome... 

Y/N : (to self) That’s odd...he’s usually so...(pauses) so...cheerful... 

Y/N : Hey Jackson-? 

(Jackson cuts them off by turning and walking away, as if he could not be bothered by this anymore) 

Y/N: (to self) What? 

(Y/N shakes their head and continues onto their next class, Advanced Math. As they walk, they notice none of the students are moving) 

(Sound: Complete dead silence throughout the hallway, no scuffing shoes, no quiet whispers, nothing) 

Y/N : Demitra? Landon? 

(Y/N shakes the students but no reaction from either. Y/N looks concerned.) 

Y/N : (tension) What...? 

(Right as they say that, the noise resumes, as if a light switch in them flipped on again) 

(Sound: Loud conversations, bustling footsteps) 

(As Y/N is continuing their way to Advanced Math, a teacher stops them) 

Teacher: (flatly) Y/N are you alright? You’re looking a bit pale. 

Y/N: I’m fine, just tired... 

Teacher:(flatly) Whatever do you mean Y/N? 

Y/N: (shakes their head) Never mind...I better get going. 

(Y/N decides to ignore the weird phenomena happening and continued their regular routine. They go through Advanced Math, Art, and History) 

 

Scene 2 Act 1 

Interior: Passing period between Y/N’s Art and History classes) 

(The familiarity of Y/N’s schedule calms them. Everything looked normal now, and the other students seemed unconcerned) 

(Sound: Relatively normal, bustling footsteps, sneakers squeaking, conversations being made) 

(Y/N lets out a sigh of relief) 

Y/N: (to self/ relaxed) So it really was just my imagination... 

(A teacher stops Y/N abruptly)  

Teacher 2: (flatly) Y/N are you alright? You look a little pale. 

Y/N: Didn’t I already tell you I’m fine? 

Teacher 2: (flatly) What? 

Y/N: It’s just you look exactly like...the teacher that stopped me...in first period. 

(Teacher 2 just stares at them with unblinking eyes)  

Y/N: (confused)You even sound like her-? 

(As Y/N was talking, they were interrupted by the loud announcement)  

(Sound: Blaring bell, conversations hushed) 

Announcement: (calm but also serious) Attention all students, attention all students, be safe- 

(No other explanation was given by the announcement, though it sounded like it was cut off) 

(Sound: uncomfortable silence) 

Y/N: What’s happening- 

(Cut to black) 

The end... or is it?
