Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Angela Arevalo

Drama Version: "Before First Period"

Interior: School hallway, early in the morning. Gina (14) walks down a cold, empty hallway, gripping the straps of her backpack tightly, gritting her teeth behind her lips.

(Sound: Lights flickering, footsteps and far-away sounds of lockers closing)

Gina: (muttering to self) Why is it so... quiet?...

Gina looks around, her eyes tracing for anyone in the empty hallway.

(Sound: student chatter and footsteps coming closer to Gina)

Gina: (joyed to see the group) Oh hey guys! How was y'all's morning?

(Sound: group of girls continue to chatter, one girl responds to Gina)

Girl: (Bluntly, not fully paying attention) oh hey, yea good morning.

The groups of girls pass Gina, leaving her behind with a brief response and alone. It seems as if they didn't care to talk to her, rather rushed.

(Sound: The group of girls passing down the hallway, their footsteps getting quicker and more faint.)

Gina looks behind her, watching the girls turn a corner and disappear from her sight, continuing her path down the hallway.

(Sound: Footsteps, seemingly one person)

A teacher, Mr. Colt(34), comes around the corner, smiling ear to ear.

Gina: Good morning Mr. Colt! Had your morning coffee yet?

Gina teased, expecting a teasing answer back, but Mr. Colt didn't look at her, still smiling

Mr. Colt: (cheerful) Good Morning!

Mr. Colt walked past and turned a corner behind her, just like the group of girls.

Gina: (confusion) Wha- Mr. Colt? Where are you going?

Gina tries to go follow Mr. Colt, but when she turned around to follow, a familiar voice came behind her.

Mr. Colt: (cheerful) Good Morning!

Gina: Wait- but you just passed me and- hold on! You just said the exact same thing!

Mr. Colt didn't say anything, but just pass her again, following the same path as the rest.

Gina's heart pounds in her chest, not knowing what's happening to her.

Gina: (fear in her voice, shaky) what's happening...? Why was there two Mr. Colt...s?

(Sound: sudden loud alarm, the announcements turn on with the three dings.

Announcements: WARNING-LOCKDOWN. Stay in your classrooms and hide. Sighting of doublegangers were found in this school. If any of them see you, don't run. They will chase you. Don't listen of follow any of them either. Hide and stay away at all costs. WARNING-LOCK DOwn...

The announcements loop a couple times before turning off. Gina was basically sweating in fear, her breathing was harsh.

Gina: (Shaky and scared) Double what-..? Hide?... Oh no...

(Sound: Gina's ears ringing from the fear and the loud alarm in the background...)

All Gina can think of was what was going to happen to her... Was she going to live? Was she going to die? What's happening to Mr. Colt?

(Sound: Slow, wet footsteps. low growling behind Gina)

Mr. Colt(...?): Good morning, Gina.

(CUTS TO BLACK)
