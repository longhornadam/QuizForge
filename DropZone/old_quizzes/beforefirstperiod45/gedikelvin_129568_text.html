Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Elvin Gedik

CHARACTERS: 

EMMA (16)

MAX (17)

BECKY (16)

RAIN (17)

ADLAM (45)

MRS. BURKINS (34)

ANNOUNCEMENT SPEAKER

 UNKNOWN FIGURE (  ?   )

 

 

INTERIOR: EMMA walks around Emerson High's hallways at a slow tempo. Her heavy backpack slows her down, forcing her to walk slow

She never heard of her school so quiet before. Echoes bounced off the stone walls, the kind of silence that dared you to make a sound.

Sound: Small steps of Emma’s shoes and the quiet morning of a Tuesday.

EMMA: (Whispers to herself) What is going on today? It was perfectly fine yesterday…

(She approaches a tiny group filled with other juniors.)

EMMA: Hi

(The group looks at her, and they ignore her)

EMMA: (confident) School doesn’t feel like school anymore.

(The group look at her again, but BECKY says something)

BECKY: Yeah. Anyways….

(The group leaves her, walking to the cafeteria)

(EMMA decided to say hello to her teacher since no one was responding to her.)

EMMA: Good morning, Mr. Adlam!

  1. ADLAM: (blunt tone) Good morning Emma.

EMMA: What are we learning today?

  1. ADLAM: We wont.

(The weird tone in Mr. Adlam’s voice spooked Emma, as if there is something wrong with her)

EMMA: (worried) I…I don’t understand.

(Emma walked to another classroom, expecting to see her cheery science teacher to say something normal.)

EMMA: (trying to be cheerful with all the confusion) Hi Mrs. Burkins! What are we learning today?

MRS. BURKINS: (Same dry tone as Mr. Adlam) You will see.

(MRS. BURKINS immediately left her in the hallway, closed and locked her door.)

(EMMA started to really worry now. But luckily, she sees her best friend RAIN in the hallways)

EMMA: I’m having a bad morning, Rain.

RAIN: (blunt) Ok.

EMMA: (whispers quietly to herself) What is going on with people!

RAIN: You.

EMMA: (didn’t hear it) Rain? Are you okay? You’re never like this!

RAIN: You are being schizophrenic. I’m. always. like. this.

(Sound: Eerie screeching)

(RAIN’s cried black ink, EMMA had an instinct to run.)

(EMMA ran as fast as she could. The bell rang soon after that.)

ANNOUNCEMENT SPEAKER: (faint worry in their voice) Attention Emerson High students. This is not a drill. Code Lockdown. I repeat, code Lockdown.

(EMMA quickly walked towards her homeroom.)

(Her homeroom was in the third floor of the school. She climbed a flight of narrow steps from the second floor)

(A strange figure is shown as EMMA gets closer to her classroom door. The lights flicker on and off, as if the third floor was haunted)

(Sound: Light buzz)

(Students rushed one by one into the classroom. MAX and BECKY seemed unbothered, as if this was a drill.)

EMMA: Max..

MAX: (flat tone) Yeah?

EMMA: (scared) Do you wonder why we have a lockdown?

MAX: We got a threat by this unknown figure

EMMA: Unknown figure ? Like a mythologic creature?

MAX: I think. It might not be. Or it could be just an identified person.

EMMA: (starting to get angry over him being calm) And this doesn’t bother you?

MAX: Pretty much.

(EMMA stopped talking and heard a noise up from the ceiling.)

EMMA: Do you hear that?

MAX: Hear what?

(Sound: Aggressive mysterious noise from the ceiling)

(She looked around the room. Maybe BECKY heard it.)

EMMA: (stressed) Becky…do you hear that.

BECKY: No. I don’t hear anything. You are such a delusional freak.

EMMA: You don’t hear anything?! What is going on! Max? Please tell me you-

(Emma gasped at the sight of his blood on the floor, dripping away from the door, forming footstep-like clue)

EMMA: Max..?

EMMA: (uncomfortable) Mr. Keys, a student is gone! Look, it’s his blood. Mr. Keys?

  1. KEYS: Nothing is wrong with Max. He went to the bathroom.

EMMA: But you aren’t supposed to go to the bathroom at lockdown! Why would you let him go?

(Silence filled the classroom longer than the scattered talking.)

EMMA: (thinking) Can I go?

  1. KEYS: (eerie tone) Go quickly. Be careful or they will see you.

EMMA: (unlocks door) Thank you.

(She follows the red path to doom. She needed answers. No one was answering her.)

(Sound: Rapid, soft footsteps)

(She finds a tall, UNKNOWN FIGURE who is dragging a body away, smearing blood around the marble floor down the main hall)

EMMA: Max…

(Blackout)

END.
