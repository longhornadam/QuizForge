Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Grant Edwards

Drama Version: "Before First Period"

Interior: School hallway, early morning. Marcia (14) walks along a back hallway with her books clutched in her arms protectively.

(Sound: Lockers clanging in the distance. Footsteps echo,)

Justin: (quietly - to self) Why does it feel so empty?

(Justin, walking down the hallway.)

Justin: Hey, what are you doing John? Why does it feel so empty?

John: (no expression) I don't know know. It doesn't feel empty to me.

(John walks away at a fast pace, making the interaction feel rushed.)

Justin: What's going on with everybody?

(Sound: The hallway noise fades.)

(A few moments later, Justin has his first interaction with his teacher.)

Justin: (walking into class) Hi Mrs. Johnson, how is your day going?

Mrs. Johnson: (no expression) I'm okay.

Justin: Is everything okay?

Mrs. Johnson: Everything's fine.

(The conversation ended abruptly, with no real resolution)

(JUSTIN had noticed that both John and Mrs. Johnson has almost the exact same body movements, and they both had the same blank facial expression.)

Justin: Something has happened to this school and everybody in it, I just don't know what.

Sound: (Distant footsteps echoing)

(Justin was heading to his second period class when he encountered another adult that looked almost exactly identical with the first teacher.)

Justin: I just - I just... I just say you down at the other side of the hallway.

Sophia: I don't know what you're talking about.

(Sophia suddenly walked away without saying another word.)

(Justin finally gets to his second period class with Mr. James)

Mr. James: Okay class, let's open up to page forty seven of our notebooks.

(Mr. James seemed to be the only normal person Justin met today)

(Sound: Pages shuffling, Pencils clattering.)

(As Mr.James was talking, a school announcement came on.)

(Announcement: Everybody must stay in the building until told to leave)

Justin: What's going on? Am I going crazy?

(Sound: Complete silence. Everybody's sitting perfectly still.)

Mr. James: Come with me Justin. I've been waiting for this for a long time.

(What JUSTIN didn't know was that nobody was acting as their own individuals anymore, but as being guided away.)
