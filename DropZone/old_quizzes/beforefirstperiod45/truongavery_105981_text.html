Before First Period (Adaptation - Major): Avery Truong

Before first period

Interior: Berkley High school hallway, 6:45 in the morning, Sadie (18) walking down from tutoring to go to the gym.

(Sound: Heavy footsteps echoing though the senior hallways while the kids walk by silently)

Sadie: (to herself) It feels empty even though I see like 100 different people.

(tiny bunches of kids speaking very loudly)

(Sadie sees her friends and goes up to them)

Sadie: Hiiiiii are we still on for basketball tomorrow, Hally?

Hally: no sorry, got homework

(Halley and rest of group start giggling)

(Sadie walks away in humiliation)

Sadie: (says to herself) what do you mean you have homework we have all the same classes, and we haven’t even started the day.

(female teacher comes from the music room)

Sadie: Hi, Mrs. Willow  

Mrs. Willow: (In a high-pitched voice with a straight face) Hallow Sadie.

(a cold breeze hits Sadie)

Sadie: (to herself) That was weird, Mrs. Willow usually has a very deep voice, it is probably just a voice crack.

(Sadie is halfway to the gym Coach A is standing and for a split-second when Coach A turns around Sadie sees Mrs. Willow)

(Anyways, she goes up to Coach A)

Sadie: Hii Coach A, when are basketball tryouts?

Coach A: (With a straight face and a high-pitched voice) Hello Sadie, and it’s September 13.

(Sadie walking past coach in a nervous strut as she speeds up)

Sadie: that got to be a coincidence….

(7:00 announcements come on)

(random screech coming from the speaker)

Hally: (In a soft voice) Everyone please make sure to have your plant supplies on…….

(announcements cut off briefly)

(kids running to the nearest exit very silently)

Sadie: (in her mind as she is sprinting to exit 66B) plant is our school’s safe word, but what else was she going to say? (in a worried panic)

(Sadie watching as all the adults surround the kids and guide them away into the gym)
